Design for Life.

Inspired by humanity, we cultivate designs with the power to enrich human experiences and transform the urban landscape.
We are LA ARCHITECTS , an interdisciplinary architecture and design firm built on an 2020-year foundation of experience and dedicated to the pursuit of design excellence with a deep focus on sustainability and human connection.
EL MOLINÓN

El Molinon is a new residential project being developed in the Monterrey, a vibrant zone that is a landmark in Mexico’s north-east for its rapid urban and commercial expansion.

LA MANCHA

A residential development on the Riviera Maya, used its significant geographical (jungle) and historical (pre-Hispanic Mayan culture) context as a conceptual foundation to allow an extensive residential masterplan to consciously insert itself into a territory of unique qualities.

AKUMAL

Open spaces are essential for fostering social interactions and creating a dynamic, welcoming environment. These spaces not only facilitate cross-ventilation but also contribute to energy conservation.

COUNTRY

The project involves the transformation of a former office into a residential space. The primary objective is to create a seamless experience of spatial transitions, particularly from public to private areas.
